About Our Server

Welcome to PhrustV2, a survival Minecraft server with a friendly community and exciting features. Our server offers:

  • Survival gameplay with land claiming
  • Economy system with player shops
  • Regular events and competitions
  • Friendly and active staff team
  • Custom plugins for enhanced gameplay
  • Unraidable cave system for dedicated players

Join us today and become part of our growing community!

Unraidable Cave System

Our server features a unique unraidable cave system for dedicated players. To request an unraidable cave:

  1. Navigate to the "Unraidable Cave" page
  2. Explain why you need an unraidable cave
  3. Submit your request
  4. Our staff will review and respond via Discord

Cooldown: You can only submit one request every 24 hours.
